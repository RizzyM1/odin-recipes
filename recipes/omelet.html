Omelet

Description

Starting off your day with a veggie omelet is perfection. Each bite is loaded with perfectly cooked veggies and cheese. Enjoy this with a hot cup of coffee, or whip it up as a quick brunch recipe as well! It is easy to make as well by following 6 quick steps

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Veggies

    • Steps

    1. Add the prepared veggies (minus the spinach) to a hot skillet, with a little bit of oil.
    2. Add in the spinach and sautee.
    3. Remove the cooked veggies from the pan and add the eggs.
    4. Cook until the omelet starts to look done, then add the cheese and cooked vegetables to one half of the egg.
    5. Fold the top half of the egg over the top.
    6. Cook until the cheese is melted.
