Chicken Sandwich

Description

Never underestimate the power of great chicken sandwiches! Excellent platter for gatherings, picnics and work lunches. My secrets? Fresh dill, a handful of walnuts and shred the chicken really finely using a stand-mixer or electric beater.

If you've ever wondered why those chicken sandwiches from “that gourmet deli” are so coveted and command such high prices, the answer lies not just in the secret mix of ingredients for the filling but also in the texture of the chicken.

The secret? Shredding the chicken super, super finely, until it’s basically crumbly, using a stand-mixer. This makes the chicken sandwich filling soft and creamy because you don't have chewy chunks of chicken in it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sour Cream
  • Walnuts
  • Fresh Dill
  • Pickles
  • Celery and green onion
  • Onion, garlic, salt, pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Dijon mustard

    • Steps

    1. Remove flesh off the chicken and remove skin.
    2. Shred the chicken as finely as you can.
    3. Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
    4. Pour sauce over the chicken.
    5. Butter the Bread.
    6. Spread the filling on the bread, from edge to edge.
    7. Cut off crusts
    8. Cut in half on the diagonal, or into rectangles, then line them upon a platter!
